# User Guide

## Introduction

Substracker is a mobile application designed to help users manage and monitor their digital subscription services efficiently.

---

## Login

1. Open the application.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Tap **Sign In**.

---

## Dashboard

The dashboard displays:

- Total monthly subscription expenses
- Active subscriptions
- Upcoming payments
- Recent subscription list

---

## Add Subscription

To add a subscription:

1. Tap the **+** button.
2. Fill in the subscription information.
3. Tap **Save**.

---

## Edit Subscription

1. Open a subscription.
2. Select **Edit**.
3. Update the required information.
4. Save the changes.

---

## Delete Subscription

1. Open the subscription details.
2. Select **Delete**.
3. Confirm the deletion.

---

## Notes

All subscription data is stored locally using SQLite and remains available even without an internet connection.
